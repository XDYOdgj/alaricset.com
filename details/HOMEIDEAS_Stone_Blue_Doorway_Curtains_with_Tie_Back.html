Elegant Privacy with Functional Design

The HOMEIDEAS Stone Blue Doorway Curtains are a stylish and practical addition to any room. Designed to offer both privacy and light control, these curtains are perfect for separating spaces like bedrooms, closets, or doorways. Their solid color design blends seamlessly into your decor, adding a touch of sophistication while maintaining a clean and modern look.

With a durable 100% polyester construction, these panels are built to last and provide excellent blackout performance, blocking up to 95% of light and harmful UV rays. This helps protect your furniture and improves energy efficiency by keeping your space at a comfortable temperature. The grommet top design makes installation effortless, allowing the curtains to slide smoothly along your rod with no hassle.

Featuring an adjustable thermal tie-back, these curtains let you customize the tension to secure your space while enhancing its appearance. Whether you need a simple door cover or a decorative element that adds both function and style, these panels are the ideal choice for your home.
